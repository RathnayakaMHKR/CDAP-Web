Milestones

In the project management framework, a milestone is a marker to mark specific points which signifies the changes or stages in development process within the project timeline. Start date, end date, inputs, budget are the some of specific points of it. In many instances, milestones do not impact with the project time duration. The main goal is to achieve the success on major progress points.
